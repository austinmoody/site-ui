Steps to exchange Direct messages with the Sandbox:

Publish your Direct certificates (Organization or Address bound) per the Direct specificationsWeb Site Disclaimers .

  1. Download the Trust Anchor for the Sandbox(direct.sitenv.org Certificate) and import the trust anchor into your trust store.
  2. Upload your Trust Anchor using the Upload Trust Anchor feature below. The Trust Anchors must be in a ".der" format.
  3. Use the information in the Send Direct Message feature below to send messages from your system to the sandbox.
  4. Use the information in the Receive Direct Message feature below to receive messages from the sandbox to your system. 

If you encounter any issues using the SITE Direct Transport Sandbox send an email to TestingServices@sitenv.org
